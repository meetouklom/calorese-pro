Calorie Calculator for Weight Loss

This tool is designed to help you calculate the number of calories you should consume each day, based on your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). It is highly recommended for those who want to lose weight, maintain a healthy lifestyle, or manage their daily calorie intake effectively.



What is BMR?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the amount of energy your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions—such as breathing, circulating blood, and maintaining body temperature—while at complete rest. Knowing your BMR helps you understand how much energy your body uses just to stay alive, even without physical activity.



What is TDEE?

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is the total amount of energy you burn in a day. It includes:
• Your BMR
• Plus the calories burned from daily activities and exercise

TDEE helps you estimate how many calories you should consume each day to:
• Maintain your current weight
• Lose weight
• Gain weight, depending on your goals



Who is this for?

This tool is perfect for:
• People who want to lose weight or control their calorie intake
• Fitness enthusiasts tracking their diet
• Anyone looking to improve their health with better nutritional planning



Tips for Use?

This tool is perfect for:
• If your goal is to lose weight, aim to consume about 300–500 fewer calories per day than your TDEE.
• Combining calorie control with regular exercise provides the best and healthiest results.
